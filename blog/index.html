Thinking Style and Learning

According to Gregorc's Mind Styles model, I am a concrete sequential thinker. A concrete perceptual quality means that I register information through my senses for what they are; I don't look for hidden meanings. A sequential ordering ability means that my mind organizes information in a linear fashion, ie. a series of steps.
